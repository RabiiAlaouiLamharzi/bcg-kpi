What This Means for You
Capacity risk is building. Act before the gap becomes critical.
Data creation is outpacing storage growth by 4 percentage points per year. Without intervention, a structural shortfall is likely by 2024 or 2025. Starting capacity planning conversations this quarter is strongly advisable.
Immediate attention needed
Redirect investment toward Object storage
Object storage is the fastest-growing segment at 10% per year. It handles AI workloads and large unstructured files more efficiently and at lower cost. File storage growth has turned negative. Reallocating budget now is well-timed.
Strategic opportunity
Review your on-premise contracts in light of cloud growth
Public cloud now represents 41% of enterprise IT spend, up from 36% in 2019. On-premise storage is steadily losing share. This is a reasonable moment to reassess existing contracts and evaluate whether a hybrid cloud model could reduce long-term costs.
